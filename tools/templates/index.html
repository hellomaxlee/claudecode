Overview
Scoring Weights
Max Slope 50%
Avg Slope 35%
Vertical Drop 15%
Total: 100%
NSAA basis: Trails are officially rated by their steepest section (max slope) — making it the primary signal. Average slope captures sustained difficulty. Vertical drop reflects stamina but is not a direct NSAA classification criterion.

Defaults: Max 50% · Avg 35% · Drop 15%
Classification Method
Computed ratings use percentile thresholds matched to the official distribution — so the same proportion of trails receive each computed tier as exists in Sugarbush's official ratings.

Official data from OpenStreetMap, mapped to NSAA standard: novice/easy → Green, intermediate → Blue, advanced → Black, expert → Double Black.
